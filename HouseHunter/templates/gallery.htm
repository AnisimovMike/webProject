Commercial real estate - buildings, structures or land used for commercial activities with the subsequent extraction of permanent profits or capital gains, rental income, investment income.

COUNTRY REAL ESTATE

Country real estate is a house with a plot near the city limits or outside the city. But this definition is too arbitrary. Today, country real estate refers to land plots, private houses, apartments and other objects that are located in small villages or suburbs, satellite towns of large cities.
